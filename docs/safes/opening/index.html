Procedures

Safe opening begins with non-destructive methods: manipulation, scope work, and electronic bypass. Forced entry is used only when non-destructive methods fail or are not cost-effective. All procedures follow manufacturer specifications where available.

Common Scenarios

Lost combinations, failed electronic locks, jammed mechanisms, relocker activation, fire damage, and estate access. Each scenario requires specific tools and procedures.
