Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to open a safe?

Safe opening costs vary depending on the type, size, and lock mechanism. Most residential safe openings start at $150-$300. Commercial and high-security safes may cost more. We provide free estimates over the phone.

Can you open my safe without damaging it?

In most cases, yes. We specialize in non-destructive safe opening techniques including manipulation, scoping, and decoding. Drilling is only used as a last resort, and we can repair the safe afterward.

How long does it take to open a safe?

Simple safes can be opened in 30 minutes to an hour. High-security safes may take 2-4 hours. We'll give you a time estimate before we begin.

Do you service commercial properties?

Yes! We work with property managers, businesses, banks, and government facilities. Services include master key systems, access control, door hardware, exit devices, and high-security locks.

What areas do you serve?

We serve a 150-mile radius from San Diego, including all of San Diego County, Riverside County, and Orange County. Cities include Vista, Oceanside, Carlsbad, Escondido, Temecula, Murrieta, and many more.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. SafeCracker Inc is a California-licensed locksmith company (LCO 6687), fully insured and bonded. We've been in business since 2010.

Do you offer emergency services?

We offer same-day and next-day service for most situations. Call us at (619) 794-8355 to discuss your needs and we'll do our best to accommodate urgent requests.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit cards, checks, and Zelle. Payment is due upon completion of service.
