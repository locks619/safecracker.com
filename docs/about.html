About SafeCracker Inc

California-licensed locksmith company serving Southern California

Professional Licensing

SafeCracker Inc holds California Locksmith Company License LCO 6687, which authorizes us to perform:

  • Safe and vault opening and servicing
  • Commercial locksmith services
  • Residential locksmith work
  • Master key systems
  • Access control installation and repair

All work is performed by licensed, experienced technicians trained in modern locksmith and safe opening techniques.

Insurance & Bonding

SafeCracker Inc is fully insured and bonded for your protection.

We carry liability insurance and are bonded as required by California law.

All work is backed by our 90-day service warranty.

Our Approach

SafeCracker Inc operates as a mobile-only service by design. This allows us to provide faster response times, competitive pricing, and focused service without the overhead of a storefront. All work is performed by licensed, insured professionals serving Southern California.

We specialize in:

  • Transparent pricing with written estimates
  • Mobile service for residential and commercial clients
  • Specialized expertise in safes, vaults, and access control
  • Professional standards and customer service
